LIVE STUDIO

I had the chance to work in a very big and furnished tv studio in my second and third year of University. I saw myself involved in different roles and tasks such as working in a live environment and having to deal with a wide crew. I loved working in a tv studio because I found the live environment very challenging as you have to deal with different tasks while the show is actually happening in front of your eyes. It's exciting because there is no room for mistakes as the audience is watching while you work on the shots.

"Ask Me" is a multicamera student project made for the University of Westminster. In this talk show different guests and games are involved. I worked as a camera operator in this project.

"Night Vision" is a student project for the University of Westminster. In this talk show different interviews and games are involved. We built the set following the halloween theme and every part of it was made as if the show was an halloween spin-off of a wider program. I worked as camera operator and editor on this show.
