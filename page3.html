DOCUMENTARIES

Here you can find my latest documentaries, where I saw myself involved in different roles such as Director and DOP. I like doing documentaries because I think that being able to express a concept or show a story through the camera lenses is very effective and it can be a very powerful way to teach something or to make people aware of something that exists and that happens everyday around them.

"Art of Adaptation: A Moses Concas Story" is a journey into the life of the very talented and famous artist Moses Concas. In this project I worked as DOP and co-director. 

"RAVE SCENE + : Raving out of the Closet" is the latest documentary I worked on. In this project we showed an insight of the LGBTQ+ Underground rave scene in London. We included three famous female djs, who gave us their point of view on the whole scene, as well as sharing with us their stories and their art. I worked as co-director and DOP on this documentary.
