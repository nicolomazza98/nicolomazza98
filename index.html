ABOUT ME


I am Nicolo', Italian guy based in London passionate about videography. I started my journey into television and videography three years ago, when I started the TV Production courses (BA) at the University of Westminster (London). During my student years I developed a wide range of skills, covering different roles in both university and personal projects. I like to work in all the fields of television, with a particular interest for the live sports environment. I have worked for famous music artists such as Moses Concas (Italy's Got Talent winner) and Davide Shorty (Enzo Jannacci Award winner) and I have also participated to some promotional videos for the very talented fashion artist Danielle Hackett. I am currently working as videographer for a sports company called WDSPORTZ, for which I film and edit Sunday League football matches. I love the live broadcasting environment as I think it's the most challenging and interesting part of television for me, but I also find myself very comfortable in working in documentaries and short movies. I own a Panasonic HC-X2000 camcorder with which I film most of my projects.
