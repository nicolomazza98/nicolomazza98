SPORTS

In November 2022 I have started working for a sports company called WDSPORTZ. The company focuses on providing the teams (mostly football) full match and highlights videos in order for them to watch and study them to enrich their teams' performances. I film the matches and edit them into 6-8 minutes highlights videos. I find it very interesting and it is challenging for me as you never know what can happen during a live game and I can't miss anything because otherwise the whole product will lose its utility for the managers and for the players.
